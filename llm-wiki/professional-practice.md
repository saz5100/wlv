# Professional Practice

## Professional Skills for the Workplace

### Communication
- **Verbal communication**: Presenting ideas clearly in meetings, presentations, and discussions
- **Written communication**: Producing professional emails, reports, and documentation
- **Active listening**: Understanding others' perspectives and responding appropriately
- **Non-verbal communication**: Awareness of body language, tone, and professional presence
- **Digital communication**: Using professional tools for virtual meetings and collaboration

### Teamwork
- **Collaborative working**: Contributing effectively to group projects and team activities
- **Roles and responsibilities**: Understanding team dynamics and your role within a group
- **Conflict resolution**: Managing disagreements constructively and finding solutions
- **Diversity and inclusion**: Working effectively with people from different backgrounds
- **Feedback culture**: Giving and receiving constructive feedback professionally

### Leadership
- **Leadership styles**: Understanding different approaches to leading teams and projects
- **Decision-making**: Making informed choices and taking responsibility for outcomes
- **Motivation and delegation**: Inspiring others and distributing tasks effectively
- **Strategic thinking**: Seeing the bigger picture and planning for long-term goals
- **Resilience**: Maintaining composure and effectiveness under pressure

### Ethical Practice
- **Professional ethics**: Understanding codes of conduct and ethical standards in your field
- **Integrity**: Being honest, transparent, and accountable in professional activities
- **Confidentiality**: Handling sensitive information appropriately
- **Sustainability**: Considering environmental and social impact in professional decisions
- **Equality and diversity**: Promoting fair treatment and equal opportunities in the workplace
