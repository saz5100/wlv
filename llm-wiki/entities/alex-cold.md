# Alex Cold

Alex Cold is the fifteen-year-old protagonist of the extract from *City of the Beasts*. He is depicted as being in a state of emotional fragility, struggling to process his mother's illness.

### Character Traits and Situation
- **Emotional State:** He experiences nightmares and a sense of dread, describing some days as 'terrible' where 'everything is going to turn out bad'.
- **Family Dynamics:** He feels a sense of frustration and anger towards his father's inadequacy and his younger sisters' bluntness regarding their mother's health.
- **Grief and Loss:** He deeply misses his mother's presence, laughter, and discipline, viewing her current state as that of a 'silent ghost'.
