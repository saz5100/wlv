# YouTube Video Guides Index

This page indexes YouTube video guides identified in the [[search-lan-research-log]] as supplementary resources for AQA Paper 1 revision. These videos are best used as supplementary material alongside the wiki's written guides and model answers.

## Videos Found

### Q5 — Creative Writing
- **How to get 40/40 in Q5** — General strategy guide for achieving full marks on the writing task.
- **How to Get a 9 in GCSE English Language** — Broader guide covering multiple questions including Q5.

### Q2 — Language Analysis
- **Q2 Grade 9 in Real Time (Mr Salles)** — Real-time walkthrough of a Grade 9 Q2 response, demonstrating analytical technique and time management.

### Q3 — Structure Analysis
- **Q3 Structure Explained** — Overview of structural features and how to analyse them.
- **AQA English Language Paper 1 Q3 Grade 9** — Exemplar Grade 9 response for the structure question.
