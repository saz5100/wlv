# Save My Exams Resource Index

Save My Exams is a commercial revision platform providing model answers, revision notes, and how-to guides for AQA GCSE English Language Paper 1 (8700). It was the most comprehensive single source identified in the [[search-lan-research-log]].

## Resources

### Q5 — Creative Writing
- **Q5 Narrative Writing Model Answer:** Exemplar response demonstrating narrative structure, characterisation, and descriptive techniques at a Grade 9 standard.
- **Q5 Descriptive Writing Model Answer:** Exemplar response focusing on sensory detail, imagery, and atmospheric description.
- **Q5 Creative Writing Revision Notes:** Guidance on planning, structuring, and crafting effective creative writing responses.

### Q2 — Language Analysis
- **Q2 Language Analysis Model Answer:** Annotated exemplar showing how to analyse vocabulary, imagery, and sensory details using the what-how-why framework.
- **Q2 How to Answer:** Step-by-step guide to approaching the language analysis question, including selection of evidence and structuring analysis.

### Q3 — Structure Analysis
- **Q3 Structure Model Answer:** Exemplar response demonstrating analysis of narrative focus shifts, structural devices, and their effects.
- **Q3 Structure Analysis Skills:** Guidance on identifying and analysing structural features such as openings, endings, juxtaposition, and chronological vs non-linear structure.

### Overview & Timing
- **Paper 1 Overview & Timings:** Breakdown of the AQA Paper 1 format, question weightings, and recommended time allocations.

## WLV Adaptation Notes

All Save My Exams resources are designed for the full AQA Paper 1 (1 hour 45 minutes, including Q4 and writing choice). For WLV Equivalency test preparation, the following adaptations are required:
- **Q4 content should be ignored** — WLV removes the evaluation question.
- **Timing advice should be adjusted** — WLV is 2 hours; AQA timing (e.g., 45 minutes for Q5) may need recalibration.
- **Writing choice is not available** — WLV uses a fixed thematic prompt; resources assuming choice should be used for technique only.
- **Single-source extraction** — WLV uses one extract for all reading questions; resources referencing multiple sources are not directly applicable.

## Quality Note
