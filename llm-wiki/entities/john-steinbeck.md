# John Steinbeck

John Steinbeck was a prominent 20th-century American author known for his depictions of the working class and the social struggles of the Great Depression era.

## Connection to Assessment
Steinbeck's novel *The Pearl* (1947) was selected as the source text for the **AQA Paper 1 June 2022** examination. His writing is typically characterised by detailed sensory descriptions and a focus on the relationship between humans and their environment, making his work ideal for [[language-analysis-q2]] and [[structural-analysis-q3]].
