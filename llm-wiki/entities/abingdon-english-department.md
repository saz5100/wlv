# Abingdon English Department

The **Abingdon English Department** is the academic body responsible for the production of the *Pocket Guide to Grammar, Punctuation and Spelling*. Their materials provide the technical grammatical standards used to support students in preparing for high-stakes English assessments, including the AQA and WLV specifications.
