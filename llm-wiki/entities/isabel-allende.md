# Isabel Allende

Isabel Allende is the author of the novel *City of the Beasts* (2004), which serves as the source text for the AQA Paper 1 mock examination. Her writing in this extract is characterised by a focus on psychological distress, familial tension, and the use of vivid imagery to convey internal emotional states.
