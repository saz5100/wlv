# Yann Martel

Yann Martel (born 1963) is a Canadian author best known for his novel *Life of Pi* (2001), which won the Man Booker Prize in 2002. An extract from *Life of Pi* was used as the source text for the AQA Paper 1 June 2023 examination.

## Literary Style

Martel's prose in *Life of Pi* is characterised by vivid sensory description, philosophical reflection, and a blend of realism and magical realism. The extract used in the AQA paper demonstrates his ability to combine scientific observation with emotional and subjective response.
