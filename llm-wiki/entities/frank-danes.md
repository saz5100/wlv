# Frank Danes

Frank Danes is the author of *The Little Book of Spelling, Punctuation and Grammar (SPaG)*, published by OCR in 2020. His work focuses on the practical application of SPaG rules, specifically targeting the common errors made by students in GCSE-level English examinations.
