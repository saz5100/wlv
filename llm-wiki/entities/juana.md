# Juana

Juana is a central character in John Steinbeck's novel *The Pearl*, appearing in the June 2022 AQA Paper 1 extract.

## Role in the Text
In the provided extract, Juana is the mother of the baby and the wife of [[kino]]. She is presented as a figure of resilience and maternal instinct.

## Character Dynamics
The text highlights the contrast between Juana's and Kino's reactions to the crisis involving their baby. In the context of Question 4 of the June 2022 paper, the writer explores the tension and differing roles the two characters adopt when facing danger, with Juana often depicted as the one attempting to secure the baby's safety.
