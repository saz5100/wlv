# Search.lan Research Log

This document records a systematic trawl of publicly available AQA Paper 1 (8700) revision resources conducted via search.lan (SearXNG) on 09/06/2026. The purpose was to identify and evaluate external materials to fill gaps in the WLV English Equivalency wiki.

## Scope

The search targeted resources for AQA GCSE English Language Paper 1 (8700) only. All resources found are AQA-specific and require adaptation for the WLV Equivalency test format (no Q4, single-source extraction, fixed thematic prompt, 2-hour timing).

## Resources Found

### Save My Exams
The most comprehensive single source, providing model answers and revision notes for Q2, Q3, and Q5, plus an overview and timing guide. Resources include:
- Q5 Narrative Writing Model Answer
- Q5 Descriptive Writing Model Answer
- Q5 Creative Writing Revision Notes
- Q2 Language Analysis Model Answer
- Q3 Structure Model Answer
- Paper 1 Overview & Timings
- Q2 How to Answer
- Q3 Structure Analysis Skills

### First Rate Tutors
One resource: 'Common Mistakes in English Language Paper 1' (Substack article). Useful for diagnostic content.

### PMT Education
One resource: 'AQA GCSE English Language Paper 1 Question 5 Guide'. Focused on Q5 strategy.

### MME Revise
Model solutions for November 2020 and November 2021 Paper 1, plus a Q5 revision page. Provides structured exemplars.

### GCSE Ninja
Grade 9 Creative Writing Examples. Useful for aspirational benchmarks.

### Scrbbly Blog
Single Grade 9 answer for a Q5 'old man' prompt. Limited scope.

### English Made Simple
Q2 Complete Grade 9 Guide and a guide to Grade 9 descriptive/narrative writing. Moderate coverage.

### Redbridge Tuition
Guide on how to get a 9 in Paper 1 Q5. Focused on top-band performance.

### Remarkable AI
AQA English Language Paper 1 Q5 Writing Guide. AI-generated content; quality unverified.

### Stoke Newington School
Step-by-step guide for Paper 1. School-produced resource.

### Knowunity
Grade 9 model answers (Paper 1 and Q5). User-generated content; variable quality.

### YouTube
Video guides covering Q2, Q3, Q5, and general Grade 9 strategies. Channels include Mr Salles and others.

### Tutopiya
Common mistakes list for AQA GCSE English Language. Useful for diagnostic content.

### Simple Study
AQA GCSE English Language model answers. Platform-based resource.

### Owl Tutors
'9 Ways to Get 9 in GCSE English Language'. General tips.

### Tutor In
AQA GCSE English Language Paper 1 Revision Guide. Comprehensive overview.

### Richard Lander School
AQA Language Supporting Booklet 2024. Includes sentence stems and tips.

## Key Observations

- **Q5 resources are the most abundant** — 10+ dedicated resources found, reflecting the high weighting of this question.
- **Q2 and Q3 have moderate coverage** — Save My Exams provides the most structured model answers; YouTube offers supplementary video guides.
- **Q1 is conspicuously absent** — No dedicated Q1 resources were sought or found, consistent with Q1 being straightforward retrieval (4 marks).
- **No Q4 resources were sought** — Consistent with WLV scope (Q4 removed).
- **All resources are AQA 8700-specific** — None are WLV-specific; all require adaptation for the WLV format.

## Open Questions

1. **Quality assessment:** Which resources are accurate and aligned with current AQA mark schemes? The log does not evaluate quality.
2. **Adaptation methodology:** How exactly were AQA resources adapted for WLV format? The synthesis process is not documented.
3. **Copyright status:** Are model answers from commercial sites (Save My Exams, MME Revise) usable without licensing issues?
4. **WLV-specific gaps:** Does the wiki now have sufficient coverage for all WLV testable skills?
5. **Resource currency:** Some URLs reference 2020/2021 papers — are these still aligned with current AQA specifications?
