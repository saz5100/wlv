# AQA Paper 1 June 2024 Mark Scheme

This document is the official mark scheme for the AQA GCSE English Language Paper 1 examination from June 2024. The source text for this paper is an extract from *The Grass is Singing* by Doris Lessing.

## Content Overview
The mark scheme provides detailed indicative content and level descriptors for the following questions:
- **Question 1**: Explicit retrieval of information regarding the character Mary Turner.
- **Question 2**: Language analysis focusing on the effects of heat.
- **Question 3**: Structural analysis of the narrative architecture and focus shifts.
- **Question 4**: Critical evaluation of the relationship between Dick and Mary Turner.
- **Question 5**: Creative writing assessment (Content and Organisation and Technical Accuracy).

## Key Assessment Objectives (AOs)
- **AO1**: Identification and interpretation of explicit and implicit information.
- **AO2**: Analysis of language and structure.
- **AO4**: Critical evaluation of texts.
- **AO5**: Communication of ideas clearly, effectively, and imaginatively.
- **AO6**: Technical accuracy in spelling, punctuation, and grammar.

## Relevance to WLV Equivalency
While this is a full AQA mark scheme, it is used in the WLV context to provide the "perceptive" standards required for high-level marks in Q1, Q2, and Q3, and the AO6 technical accuracy standards for the writing task. Note that Q4 and the writing choice in Q5 are out of scope for the WLV assessment.
