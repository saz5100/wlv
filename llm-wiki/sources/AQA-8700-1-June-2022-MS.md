# AQA Paper 1 June 2022 Mark Scheme

This document is the official mark scheme for the AQA GCSE English Language Paper 1 (June 2022) examination. It provides the criteria and indicative content used by examiners to grade responses based on an extract from *The Pearl* by John Steinbeck.

## Key Components
- **Level of Response Marking**: Utilises a four-tier "ladder" system (Level 1 to Level 4) to categorise the quality of student analysis.
- **Assessment Objectives**: Maps responses to AO1 (Retrieval), AO2 (Language and Structure), AO4 (Evaluation), and AO5/AO6 (Creative Writing).
- **Indicative Content**: Provides specific examples of what examiners were looking for in the June 2022 paper, such as the juxtaposition of the 'Song of the Family' and the 'Song of Evil'.

## WLV Equivalency Relevance
While this mark scheme follows the full AQA specification, it is critical for WLV students to note:
- **In Scope**: The marking logic for Q1, Q2, Q3, and Q5.
- **Out of Scope**: The criteria for Q4 (Critical Evaluation) and the choice between writing prompts in Q5.

The AO6 (Technical Accuracy) section is particularly relevant given the high SPaG weighting in the WLV assessment.
