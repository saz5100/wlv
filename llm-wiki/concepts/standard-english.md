# Standard English

Standard English refers to the widely accepted form of the English language that follows established rules of grammar, syntax, and spelling. It is the required medium for academic and formal writing in the WLV and AQA assessments.

## Application in AO6
Under **AO6 (Technical Accuracy)**, students are assessed on their ability to maintain Standard English consistently.

- **Consistency**: High-level responses (Level 3 and 4) demonstrate a consistent and appropriate use of Standard English.
- **Appropriateness**: While dialect or colloquialisms may be used within dialogue to establish character, the narrative voice and overall structure must adhere to Standard English rules.
- **Impact of Errors**: Frequent deviations from Standard English (e.g., incorrect subject-verb agreement or non-standard syntax) typically limit a response to Level 1 or 2.
