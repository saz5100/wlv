# Reported Speech

Reported speech (or indirect speech) is when a speaker's words are rephrased and relayed to the reader without using direct quotations.

## Characteristics
- No inverted commas are used.
- The tense often shifts backward (e.g., "I am tired" becomes *He said that he was tired*).
- Pronouns change to reflect the reporter's perspective.
- No special punctuation is required beyond the standard rules of the sentence.
