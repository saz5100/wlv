# Power Dynamics Analysis

Power dynamics analysis involves examining how control, influence, and authority shift between characters within a narrative.

## Mechanisms of Power
- **Judgement and Criminalisation**: One character acting as the "judge" while the other is cast as the "criminal" (e.g., Dick Turner's treatment of Mary).
- **Emotional Leverage**: Power shifting when one character requires forgiveness or expresses guilt.
- **Environmental Control**: How the setting (e.g., the oppressive heat in *The Grass is Singing*) can strip characters of their agency.

## Analytical Application
When analysing power dynamics, look for:
- **Verbs of Command**: Who is giving orders?
- **Silencing**: Who is prevented from speaking or being heard?
- **Pivotal Moments**: Specific phrases (e.g., "suddenly sorry") that signal a change in the hierarchy of the relationship.
