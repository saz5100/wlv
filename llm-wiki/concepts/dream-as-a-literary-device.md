# Dream as a Literary Device

The extract from *City of the Beasts* opens with a nightmare, using the dream state to externalise the protagonist's internal fears.

## Analysis of the Nightmare
- **Symbolism of the Black Bird:** The 'enormous black bird' that carries off the mother serves as a metaphor for death or illness—an unstoppable, predatory force that removes the maternal figure from the safety of the home.
- **Shattered Glass:** The 'clatter of shattered glass' symbolises the breaking of the family's peace and the fragility of their domestic security.
- **Helplessness:** Alex's role as a helpless observer in the dream mirrors his real-life inability to cure his mother or fix his family's situation.
