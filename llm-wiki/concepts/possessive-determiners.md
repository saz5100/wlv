# Possessive Determiners

Possessive determiners are words that indicate ownership but **never** take an apostrophe. Confusing these with contractions is a frequent SPaG error.

## Key Determiners
- *my, your, his, her, its, our, their, whose*

## Critical Distinction: Its vs It's
- **Its** (Possessive Determiner): Belonging to it.
  - *Example: "The dog wagged its tail."*
- **It's** (Contraction): Short for "it is" or "it has".
  - *Example: "It's raining outside."*
