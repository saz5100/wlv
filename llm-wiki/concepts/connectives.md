# Connectives

Connectives are words or phrases used to link ideas, sentences, and paragraphs, ensuring the text flows logically.

## Types of Connectives
- **Causal**: *therefore*, *consequently*, *as a result*, *thus*.
- **Contrastive**: *however*, *nevertheless*, *on the other hand*, *conversely*.
- **Additive**: *furthermore*, *moreover*, *in addition*.
- **Sequential**: *firstly*, *subsequently*, *finally*.
