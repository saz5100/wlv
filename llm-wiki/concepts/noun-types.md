# Noun Types

Nouns are categorised based on what they represent and how they are formatted.

## Categories
- **Common Nouns**: General names for things (e.g., *dog*, *city*, *table*). These are not capitalised unless they start a sentence.
- **Proper Nouns**: Specific names for people, places, or organisations (e.g., *London*, *Mary*, *Microsoft*). These must always be capitalised.
- **Concrete Nouns**: Things that can be perceived by the senses (e.g., *apple*, *rain*).
- **Abstract Nouns**: Ideas, qualities, or states (e.g., *love*, *freedom*, *courage*).
