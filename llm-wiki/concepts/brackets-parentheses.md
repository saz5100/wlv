# Brackets (Parentheses)

Brackets ( ) are used to insert non-essential, additional information or a side-note into a sentence.

## Usage Rules
- The information inside the brackets should be supplementary; if removed, the sentence must still make complete grammatical sense.
- *Example: "The capital of France (which is famous for its art) is Paris."*
