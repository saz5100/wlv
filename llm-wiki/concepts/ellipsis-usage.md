# Ellipsis Usage

An ellipsis (...) consists of three dots and is used to indicate that something has been left out or is trailing off.

## Primary Uses
1. **Trailing Off**: To show a character's speech fading away or a moment of hesitation.
   - *Example: "I don't know... maybe we should go."*
2. **Suspense**: To create a pause before a revelation.
3. **Omitted Text**: In academic writing, to show that words have been removed from a direct quote for brevity.
