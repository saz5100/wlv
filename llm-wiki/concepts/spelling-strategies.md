# Spelling Strategies

Mastering difficult spellings requires a combination of phonetic, visual, and morphological techniques.

## Recommended Techniques
- **Phonetic Pronunciation**: Saying the word exactly as it is spelled (e.g., "Wed-nes-day").
- **Mnemonics**: Creating a memory aid (e.g., "dash in a real rush hurry or else accident" for *diarrhoea*).
- **Dividing Words**: Breaking long words into smaller, manageable chunks.
- **Letter Counting**: Remembering how many letters are in a word to avoid omissions.
- **Prefix and Suffix Rules**: Understanding how adding a prefix or suffix changes the root word.
- **Morphology**: Studying the origin of the word (e.g., Latin or Greek roots).
