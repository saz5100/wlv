# AQA Writing Task Options

Question 5 on AQA Paper 1 offers students a choice between two writing tasks: a descriptive task (based on a picture prompt) or a narrative task (based on a story prompt).

## June 2023 Options

- **Option 1 (Descriptive):** Describe a zoo or wildlife park, as suggested by the picture provided.
- **Option 2 (Narrative):** Write a story about a human meeting an animal.

## Key Features

- **Choice:** Students select one of two options.
- **Picture prompt:** The descriptive option is accompanied by a photograph.
- **Story prompt:** The narrative option provides a brief scenario or opening.
- **Same mark scheme:** Both options are assessed using the same AO5 and AO6 criteria.

## Comparison with WLV

The WLV English Equivalency test uses a **fixed thematic prompt** with no choice. This is a key difference between the two assessments. See [[wlv-vs-aqa-differences]] for a full comparison.
