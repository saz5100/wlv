# Determiners and Articles

Determiners are words placed in front of a noun to make it clear what the noun refers to.

## Types of Determiners
- **Articles**:
    - **Definite**: *The* (refers to a specific noun).
    - **Indefinite**: *A*, *An* (refers to a general noun).
- **Possessive Determiners**: Words that show ownership (e.g., *my*, *your*, *his*, *her*, *its*, *our*, *their*). Note that these never take apostrophes (see [[possessive-determiners]]).
- **Demonstratives**: *This*, *that*, *these*, *those*.
- **Quantifiers**: *Some*, *many*, *few*, *all*.
