# AQA AO5 Content and Organisation

AO5 is the assessment objective for Content and Organisation in the AQA GCSE English Language writing tasks. It is worth 24 marks out of 40 for Q5 (60%).

## What AO5 Assesses

- **Communication:** Clarity, effectiveness, and imagination
- **Tone, style, and register:** Appropriate selection and adaptation for the task
- **Vocabulary:** Range, ambition, and precision
- **Linguistic devices:** Use of figurative language and rhetorical techniques
- **Structural features:** Organisation of ideas, sequencing, and pacing
- **Paragraphing:** Effective use of paragraph breaks
- **Discourse markers:** Use of linking words and phrases for cohesion

## Four-Level Descriptors

| Level | Descriptor | Key Features |
|-------|------------|--------------|
| Level 4 | Compelling, Convincing | Assuredly matched tone, extensive vocabulary, varied structural features, fluently linked paragraphs |
| Level 3 | Consistent, Clear | Clearly matched tone, varied vocabulary, effective structural features, coherent paragraphs |
| Level 2 | Some, Attempts | Some awareness of tone, some vocabulary range, some structural features, some paragraphing |
| Level 1 | Simple, Limited | Simple vocabulary, limited structural features, basic paragraphing |
