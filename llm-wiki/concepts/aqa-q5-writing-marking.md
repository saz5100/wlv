# AQA Q5 Writing Marking

Question 5 on AQA Paper 1 is a 40-mark writing task. It is assessed using two assessment objectives: AO5 (Content and Organisation, 24 marks) and AO6 (Technical Accuracy, 16 marks).

## Mark Allocation

- **AO5 (Content and Organisation):** 24 marks (60% of Q5)
- **AO6 (Technical Accuracy):** 16 marks (40% of Q5, 20% of total specification)

## AO5: Content and Organisation (24 marks)

Four-level descriptors with upper and lower bands:

| Level | Descriptor | Marks |
|-------|------------|-------|
| Level 4 | Compelling, Convincing | 19-24 |
| Level 3 | Consistent, Clear | 13-18 |
| Level 2 | Some, Attempts | 7-12 |
| Level 1 | Simple, Limited | 1-6 |

**Level 4 descriptors require:**
- 'Convincing and compelling' communication
- 'Assuredly matched' tone, style, and register
- 'Extensive and ambitious' vocabulary
- 'Varied and inventive' structural features
- 'Fluently linked paragraphs with seamlessly integrated discourse markers'

## AO6: Technical Accuracy (16 marks)

Four-level descriptors:

| Level | Descriptor | Marks |
|-------|------------|-------|
| Level 4 | Consistently accurate | 13-16 |
| Level 3 | Generally accurate | 9-12 |
| Level 2 | Some accuracy | 5-8 |
| Level 1 | Limited accuracy | 1-4 |

**Level 4 descriptors require:**
- 'Consistently secure and consistently accurate' sentence demarcation
- 'Wide range of punctuation used with a high level of accuracy'
- 'Full range of appropriate sentence forms for effect'
- 'High level of accuracy in spelling, including ambitious vocabulary'
