# Analytical Paragraph Structure

For the reading questions (Q2 and Q3), paragraphs must follow a logical internal flow to ensure a coherent argument.

## The Three-Step Framework
1. **Topic Sentence**: A clear opening sentence that introduces the main point or argument of the paragraph.
2. **Detail and Development**: The "meat" of the paragraph. This includes:
   - Evidence (embedded quotations).
   - Analysis of specific words or structural features.
   - Explanation of the effect on the reader.
3. **Rounding-off Sentence**: A concluding sentence that links the point back to the overall question or transitions to the next paragraph.
