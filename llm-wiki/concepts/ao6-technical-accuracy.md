# AO6 Technical Accuracy

Assessment Objective 6 (AO6) governs the technical execution of the writing task, focusing on spelling, punctuation, and grammar (SPaG). In the WLV specification, this constitutes a significant portion of the total marks.

## Requirements for High Marks (Level 4)
To achieve the top band (13–16 marks), a student must demonstrate:
- **Secure Sentence Demarcation**: Consistent and correct use of full stops and capital letters to mark sentence boundaries.
- **Wide Range of Punctuation**: Effective use of colons, semicolons, dashes, and commas for effect.
- **Ambitious Vocabulary**: Use of sophisticated words that enhance the precision and impact of the writing.
- **High Accuracy**: Minimal errors in spelling and grammar.

## Common Pitfalls (Level 1-2)
- **Comma Splicing**: Joining two independent clauses with only a comma.
- **Limited Punctuation**: Relying solely on full stops and commas.
- **Basic Vocabulary**: Overuse of generic adjectives (e.g., "big", "bad", "nice").
