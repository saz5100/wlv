# Exclamation Mark Usage

The exclamation mark (!) is used to convey strong emotion, urgency, or emphasis.

## Usage Guidelines
- **Creative Writing**: Effective for showing shock, anger, or excitement in dialogue.
- **Formal Writing**: Generally **inappropriate** for formal or analytical essays. Overuse can make writing appear unprofessional or overly emotional.
