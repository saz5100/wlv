# Parts of Speech

Parts of speech are classifications of words based on their function within a sentence. Understanding these is fundamental to technical accuracy and linguistic analysis.

## Classifications
- **Verbs**: Action or state words.
- **Nouns**: Words for people, places, things, or ideas (see [[noun-types]]).
- **Adjectives**: Words that describe or modify nouns.
- **Adverbs**: Words that modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs.
- **Pronouns**: Words that replace nouns (e.g., he, she, it, they).
- **Prepositions**: Words showing the relationship between a noun/pronoun and another part of the sentence (e.g., in, on, at, by).
- **Conjunctions**: Words that link words, phrases, or clauses.
- **Interjections**: Words expressing strong emotion (e.g., Wow!, Ouch!).
- **Determiners**: Words that introduce nouns (see [[determiners-and-articles]]).
