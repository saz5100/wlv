# Creative Writing Assessment (AO5)

Assessment Objective 5 (AO5) focuses on the "Content and Organisation" of the writing task (Q5). It measures how effectively a student communicates their ideas.

## Key Criteria
- **Communication**: Is the writing compelling and engaging?
- **Tone and Register**: Is the voice appropriate for the task (e.g., descriptive vs narrative)?
- **Vocabulary**: Is the word choice ambitious and precise?
- **Structural Features**: Are discourse markers used seamlessly to link ideas?

## Marking Levels
- **Level 4 (Compelling)**: Sophisticated control of tone, ambitious vocabulary, and a seamless structure.
- **Level 1 (Simple)**: Limited vocabulary, random paragraphing, and unlinked ideas.

## WLV Application
For the WLV assessment, students must produce a high-quality 400-word piece. The focus is on maintaining a consistent, sophisticated tone within this condensed limit.
