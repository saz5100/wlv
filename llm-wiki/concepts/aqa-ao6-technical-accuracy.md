# AQA AO6 Technical Accuracy

AO6 is the assessment objective for Technical Accuracy in the AQA GCSE English Language writing tasks. It is worth 16 marks out of 40 for Q5 (40%), and constitutes 20% of the total specification.

## What AO6 Assesses

- **Sentence demarcation:** Correct use of capital letters, full stops, question marks, and exclamation marks
- **Punctuation:** Range and accuracy of punctuation marks (commas, colons, semicolons, dashes, brackets, etc.)
- **Sentence forms:** Use of simple, compound, and complex sentences for effect
- **Standard English:** Consistent use of standard grammatical forms
- **Spelling:** Accuracy, including ambitious vocabulary
- **Vocabulary:** Range and precision of word choice

## Four-Level Descriptors

| Level | Descriptor | Key Features |
|-------|------------|--------------|
| Level 4 | Consistently accurate | Secure sentence demarcation, wide range of punctuation, full range of sentence forms, high spelling accuracy |
| Level 3 | Generally accurate | Mostly accurate sentence demarcation, range of punctuation, varied sentence forms, generally correct spelling |
| Level 2 | Some accuracy | Some sentence demarcation, some punctuation, some variety in sentence forms, some spelling errors |
| Level 1 | Limited accuracy | Limited sentence demarcation, limited punctuation, simple sentence forms, frequent spelling errors |
