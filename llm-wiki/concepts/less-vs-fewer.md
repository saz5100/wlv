# Less vs Fewer

The choice between 'less' and 'fewer' depends on whether the noun being described is countable or uncountable.

## Fewer (Countable)
Use 'fewer' for things that can be counted as individual units (plural nouns).
- *Example*: "There are **fewer** students in the class today."
- *Test*: If you can count them (1, 2, 3...), use 'fewer'.

## Less (Uncountable)
Use 'less' for singular nouns, mass nouns, or uncountable quantities.
- *Example*: "There is **less** water in the jug."
- *Test*: If it is a bulk amount or an abstract concept, use 'less'.
