# Illness and Family as a Theme

In the extract from *City of the Beasts*, illness is not merely a medical condition but a catalyst for the total transformation of family dynamics.

## Key Manifestations
- **The 'Silent Ghost':** The mother's illness has stripped her of her identity, turning her from a vibrant artist and disciplinarian into a shadow of her former self.
- **Role Reversal and Tension:** The father's inability to maintain the household (symbolised by the poor cooking) increases the burden on the children, leading to conflict between the siblings.
- **Atmospheric Weight:** The emotional toll of the illness is described through sensory metaphors, such as the air in the house feeling 'heavy, like being at the bottom of the sea'.
