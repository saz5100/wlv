# AQA Paper 1 November 2024 Mark Scheme

This document is the official mark scheme for the AQA GCSE English Language Paper 1 examination from November 2024. It provides the criteria and indicative content used to assess student responses based on an extract from *The Glass House* by Eve Chase.

## Key Assessment Components

### Reading Section
- **Question 1 (AO1):** Explicit retrieval of information. Marks are awarded for accurate identification of facts from a specific range of lines.
- **Question 2 (AO2):** Language analysis. Focuses on the effect of words, phrases, and sentence forms.
- **Question 3 (AO2):** Structural analysis. Examines narrative hooks, focus shifts (e.g., flashbacks), and the overall architecture of the text.
- **Question 4 (AO4):** Critical evaluation. Requires students to evaluate a specific statement and determine the extent of their agreement.

### Writing Section
- **Question 5 (AO5 & AO6):** Creative writing. Assessed on content, organisation, and technical accuracy (SPaG).

## Marking Methodology
The scheme employs a **Level of Response** marking system, ranging from Level 1 (Simple/Limited) to Level 4 (Perceptive/Detailed). Examiners follow a three-step process:
1. Annotate the response.
2. Determine the appropriate Level.
3. Assign the final mark based on the level descriptors.
