# Lesson Generation Guide: WLV English Equivalency

## 1. Core Principles & Tone
*   **Target Audience:** Adult learners. Avoid "school-style" patronising tones. Use professional, respectful, and academically rigorous language.
*   **Language Standard:** **UK English only.** (e.g., *summarise* not *summarize*, *programme* not *program*, *centre* not *center*).
*   **Primary Focus:** Academic Reading Comprehension and Formal Writing.
