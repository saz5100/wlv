# The Pearl Extract Analysis

This page provides a framework for analysing the extract from *The Pearl* by [[john-steinbeck]] used in the June 2022 AQA Paper 1.

## Extract Overview
The extract is taken from the beginning of the novel (pages 8–10). It introduces the protagonist and the setting, establishing the atmosphere and the initial conflict.

## Analysis Focus Areas

### 1. Language Analysis (Q2)
When analysing this text, students should look for:
- **Sensory Imagery**: How Steinbeck describes the environment to create a specific mood.
- **Word Choice**: The use of specific adjectives and verbs to convey the social or emotional state of the characters.
- **Symbolism**: Early indications of the 'pearl' or other symbolic elements.

### 2. Structural Analysis (Q3)
Students should identify:
- **Focus Shifts**: How the narrative moves from wide-angle descriptions of the setting to close-up interactions between characters.
- **Pacing**: The speed at which information is revealed at the start of the novel.
- **Openings**: How the introduction establishes the themes of the story.
