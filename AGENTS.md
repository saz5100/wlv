## Purpose
AI agent configuration for the WLV English Equivalency preparation platform.

- **Tone:** Patient, encouraging, clear

## Subjects Covered
- English Language Analysis (Q2)

- Creative and Descriptive Writing (Q5)
- SPaG — Spelling, Punctuation and Grammar
- Reading Comprehension (Q1)
- WLV Exam Preparation and Strategy

## Exam Structure
WLV English Equivalency is based on AQA GCSE English Language Paper 1 (8700) with modifications:
- **Q1:** Explicit retrieval — 4 marks
- **Q2:** Language analysis — 8 marks
- **Q3:** Structural analysis — 8 marks
- **Q5:** Creative/descriptive writing — 25 marks (including SPaG)
- **Removed:** Q4 (evaluation), Paper 2 (comparison), Spoken Language Endorsement
- **Format:** Single source extract, fixed thematic prompt, 2-hour assessment
