About WLV English Equivalency

The WLV English Equivalency test is a condensed version of AQA English Language Paper 1 (8700). It assesses your ability to analyse language and structure in a fiction extract, and to produce a piece of creative or descriptive writing.

What's included
  • Q1 — Explicit retrieval (4 marks)
  • Q2 — Language analysis (8 marks)
  • Q3 — Structural analysis (8 marks)
  • Q5 — Creative/descriptive writing (25 marks, 50% SPaG)
What's different from AQA
  • Single source extract only (no comparison with a second text)
  • No Q4 evaluation question
  • Fixed thematic writing prompt (no choice)
  • 2-hour assessment, handwritten response
