🎯 Phase 1: Plan Outline

Write a brief plan: identify your opening, key descriptive/narrative techniques, structure, and ending.

💡 In the exam, planning is worth ~4 marks. Show you can structure a coherent response before writing.
✏️ Phase 2: Write Draft

Write your response. Use the Writing Lab to practise, then paste your final draft here.

💡 In the exam, this is worth ~25 marks (including SPaG). Use varied sentence structures, ambitious vocabulary, and accurate punctuation.
🔍 Phase 3: Review & Refine Polish

Review your writing. Check spelling, punctuation, grammar, and vocabulary choices. Can you improve any sentences?

💡 In the exam, this shows the examiner you can evaluate and improve your own writing — worth ~4 marks.
