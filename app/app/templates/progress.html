📊 Your Progress

Track your learning across all topics — 5 question types, mastery tracking, and spaced review

📚
Lesson Quizzes

Progress tracks your lesson quiz results only — not exams. Every time you complete a lesson quiz, your answers update these stats.

🎯
Accuracy (Latest Attempt)

Shows the percentage of unique questions where your most recent attempt was correct. Multiple attempts on the same question count once.

🏆
Mastery

A question is mastered when you get it right 3+ times in a row. Topics show badges: 🏆 Gold (90%+), 🥈 Silver (75%+), 🥉 Bronze (50%+).

🧠
Review Health

Overdue / Due Today / Upcoming counts from the spaced repetition system. These are based on your lesson quiz scores and SM-2 scheduling.

📜
Question Types

Accuracy broken down by question type — Multiple Choice, True/False, Ordering, Fill in the Blank, and Multi-Select. Shows which types need more practice.

📈
Not included

Exam results are kept separate — go to the Exam or History tab to review those. Exams are a simulated test environment and don't affect mastery tracking.
